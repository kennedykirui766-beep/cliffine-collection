How Delivery Works

1

Place Order

Browse products and add items to your cart.

2

Make Payment

Pay via M-Pesa to our official paybill or till number.

3

We Dispatch

Your order is packed and handed to our courier partner.

4

Receive Item

Your order arrives at your doorstep within the estimated time.

Important Notes

  • Delivery fees are per order, not per item.
  • Nairobi orders can be picked up for free from our location.
  • Delivery times are estimates and may vary based on courier availability.
  • Risk passes to you once the courier takes possession of the package.
  • Report any issues within 24–48 hours of receiving your order.
  • Ensure your phone number and address are correct when placing an order.
