Privacy Policy

At Cliffine Collection, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information.

Welcome to Cliffine Collection. We understand that your privacy is important to you. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or make a purchase. Please read this policy carefully.

Information We Collect

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways:

  • Personal Data: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Order Information: Product details, purchase history, and order preferences.
  • Payment Details: M-Pesa transaction codes or payment confirmation messages (we do not store your PIN or full account credentials).
  • Device Data: Browser type, IP address, and device type for security and analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To process and fulfill your orders efficiently.
  • To deliver purchased products to your specified address.
  • To confirm payments and manage transactions.
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • To improve our website experience and service quality.
  • To send promotional offers and updates (only if you opt-in).

Payment Information

All transactions are processed securely via mobile money services (M-Pesa). Cliffine Collection does not store sensitive financial data like your PIN or credit card numbers.

Data Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any personal information we process. However, please also remember that we cannot guarantee that the internet itself is 100% secure.

Sharing of Information

We respect your data and do not sell your personal information.

We may share your information with third parties only in the following circumstances:

  • Delivery Partners: We share names, phone numbers, and addresses with courier services to deliver your orders.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website and conducting our business.

Cookies

Our website may use "cookies" to enhance user experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your cart items or login sessions. You may choose to set your web browser to refuse cookies, but if you do so, some parts of the site may not function properly.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request that we correct or update your personal information.
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal or legitimate business retention requirements).
  • Opt-out of marketing communications at any time.

Updates to This Policy

Cliffine Collection may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
